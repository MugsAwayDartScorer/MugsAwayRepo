Mugs Away

Dart Scoring App

I love playing darts and am interested in programming, I thought I have a go at building a dart scoring app for my use,

it turned out really well so I decided to release it.

The windows version is free and once I get to grips with Google Play

I will realease it for 99p. If you like the app or find any bugs please report them in the reviews section below.

Please subcribe for updates and Android release date.

Play 101, 301, 501, 701 and 1001 with a friend or against the Bot!

No fuss, straightforward scoring app for dart players.

Unzip to a folder of your choice
Run the Mugs Away Windows.exe
Play Darts!
