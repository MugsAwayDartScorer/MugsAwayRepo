Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Mugs Away (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and uses personal data when you visit this website or use our applications.

What data we collect

  • Name and review content submitted via the review form
  • Email address submitted for Android release updates
  • Anonymous usage data such as page views and download counts

How we use your data

  • To display user reviews on the website
  • To send updates about the Android version (email subscribers only)
  • To understand how the website and app are used and improve them

Analytics and storage

We use Google Firebase to store reviews, subscriptions, and anonymous analytics data. Firebase may process data on servers located outside the UK or EU, using appropriate safeguards.

Cookies

This website uses cookies and similar technologies for anonymous analytics only. We do not use advertising cookies or track users across other websites.

Emails

If you subscribe to updates, you will only receive emails related to the Mugs Away app. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in our emails.

Your rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for email communications
